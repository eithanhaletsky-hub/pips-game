How to Play

1

Look at the Grid

The grid is divided into colored regions. Each region has a condition that must be satisfied.

2

Place Dominoes

Click a domino to select it, then click again to rotate. Click a cell on the grid to place it. Click a placed domino to remove it.

3

Satisfy Conditions

Every region's condition must be met by the pip values placed within it.

Conditions

=N Sum of pips equals N
== All pips are equal
≠ All pips are different
< Pips increase (left-to-right, top-to-bottom)
> Pips decrease (left-to-right, top-to-bottom)
Even All pips are even numbers
Odd All pips are odd numbers
